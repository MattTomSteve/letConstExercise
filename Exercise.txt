--What is the difference between var and let?
Reassign and redeclare with var, can't redeclare with let.
--What is the difference between var and const?
Reassign and redeclare with var, can't with const.
--What is difference between let and const?
Reassign with let, can't redeclare or reassign with const.
--What is hoisting?
Variables are lifted or hoisted to the top of their scope. Var can be accessed before it is defined.
